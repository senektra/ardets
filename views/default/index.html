Development Life Cycle for Rapid Development

ARDeTS aspires to be a development life cycle technology that aims to help those working on a rapidly developing application. Inspired by needs of a rapidly developing application, ARDeTS was envisioned first as a simple tracking system. However, much of its potential was limited to manual handwork. Named first the "Development Tracking System", I tend to to take this idea where I couldn't before, using the power of a web application to automate most of what a developer need not worry about while developing.

Semantic Versioning Deployment

ARDeTS will follow a three "phase" development life cycle, leveraging SEMVER versioning. The first phase, Development, is where an application goes through the process of obtaining new features, general maintenance and optimization. The Staging phase sees that an application be tested in a controlled environment. This ensures an application will behave as expected in the real world. The final phase, Production, is where an application is shipped or deployed to the real world for actual end users to use
